# HartfordVapes - Market Research & Business Plan

## Executive Summary

Online vape sales represent a significant and growing market opportunity. This document outlines market research, product analysis, and website planning for **HartfordVapes** - a direct-to-consumer online vape retailer.

---

## Market Analysis

### Global Vape Market Size (2024-2025)
| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Global Market Size (2024) | ~$22 billion USD |
| Projected (2030) | ~$45+ billion USD |
| Annual Growth Rate | 20-30% |
| US Market Share | ~25-30% of global |

### US-Specific Data
- **US Vape Market:** ~$5-7 billion annually
- **Online Sales:** ~35-40% of total sales
- **Growth Trend:** Continued upward trajectory despite regulations
- **Consumer Base:** 18-45 age demographic, primarily

### Market Drivers
1. **Smoking Alternative** - Many transitioning from cigarettes to vapes
2. **Flavor Variety** - Consumers attracted to diverse flavors
3. **Convenience** - Online ordering + home delivery
4. **Social Media Marketing** - Influencer promotions
5. **Harm Reduction Claims** - Perceived as safer than traditional smoking

## Regulatory Considerations

### Federal Regulations (FDA)
- **Age Verification:** Must verify 21+ for all orders
- **Product Warnings:** Required on all products
- **Flavor Restrictions:** Some states have bans
- **Marketing:** Cannot target minors

### State-Specific
- Some states (CA, NJ, etc.) have flavor bans
- Some require extra licensing
- Check state-by-state compliance

### Compliance Requirements
- ✅ Age verification at checkout
- ✅ Adult signature on delivery
- ✅ Proper product labeling
- ✅ Tax collection (varies by state)

## Risk Assessment

### Challenges
1. **Regulatory Risk** - Changing laws could impact business
2. **Age Verification** - Compliance is critical
3. **Competition** - Established players dominate
